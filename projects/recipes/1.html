Recipe for Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cream/Milk
  • Seasonings
  • Potatoes

Tips:

  • Drain well: After rinsing, let them sit for about 5 minutes to completely drain them. After, put them in a warm pot for a few minutes to make sure all the liquid evaporated.
  • Mash by hand: using a hnd masher or potato ricer ensures creamier potatoes. Make sure not to overmix to avoid a gummy texture.
  • Add Butter: preferably salty butter
  • Heat the Cream or Milk: Hear the cream/milk in a small saucepan or the microwave before adding to the potatoes.

Intructions:

  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Cut them into quarters and place into cold salted water in a large pot (cold water helps to ensure they cook evenly)
  3. Boil the potatoes: boil for around 15 minutes. TO check if they're ready, use a fork to poke the potato and see if it's tender.
  4. Mash the Potatoes: Once drained, mash the potatoes with melted butter, warmed milk/cream, and salt & pepper. (see tips for recommended things to mash with)
