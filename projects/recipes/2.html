Spring Rolls

This food is also very important to my family as it's been served often ever since I was a kid. It's also a key food in family gatherings. They're loaded with flavor and are extremely healthy, so this makes the perfect food to make!

Ingredients
Vermicelli noodles
Butter lettuce
Cabbage
Carrots
Green onion, cilantro, and mint
Persian cucumbers
Rice wrapper
Fresh Herb leaves: (Cilantro, mint, and basil)
Ingredients for Dipping Sauce
2 tbsp Peanut Butter
2 tbsp Neutral cooking oil
5 tbsp Hoisin sauce
1 tsp Sambal chile paste
4 Cloved Garlic minced
3 tbsp Roasted, crushed peanuts
1/2 cup water

How to Assemble:

Keep in mind that your ingredients for the spring roll should be cut. Carrots should be cut match-stick style and the rest are pretty self explanatory

  1. Make sure that noodles are cooked according to packaging
  2. Once all ingredients are assembled, make the base by placing a few pieces of lettuce about one-third from the bottom of the circular wrapper (rice wrapper)
  3. Add the remaning ingredients. The order doesn't matter too much, just pile them on top. Try to leave the top one-third of the wrapper open for wrapping
  4. Pull the bottom of the wrapper up and over the lower portion of the filling. Try to keep the fillings compact as you roll upwards.
  5. Evelope the sides and roll it up

How to Make the Sauce

  1. Heat pan on medium heat, then add 2 tbsp of neutral cooking oil and 4 cloves of garlic. Saute until lightly browned.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients except the chile paste and mix to combine
  3. Turn up the heat to medium high, and bring to a boil
  4. Once it hits a boil immediately shut off the heat and add the 1 tsp sambal chile paste. THe sauce will thicken a lot as it cools
  5. Add the 3 tbsp of crushed peanuts on each serving
