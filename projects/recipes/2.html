Spring Rolls

This food is also very important to my family as it's been served often ever since I was a kid. It's also a key food in family gatherings. They're loaded with flavor and are extremely healthy, so this makes the perfect food to make!

Ingredients
Vermicelli noodles
Cookied shrimp or tofu
Spring roll wrappers
Carrots
Lettuce leaves
Persian cucumbers
Fresh Herb leaves: (Cilantro, mint, and basil)
