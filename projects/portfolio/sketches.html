Dan Heng and Blade Sketch

This is currently my most recent drawing, featuring two characters from the game Honkai Star Rail. This was originally supposed to be a redraw of an art I made of them previously, but I changed different aspects of the drawing, such as the poses.

The drawing itself is supposed to highlight the lore between them. Dan Heng being the top character committed a major crime which caused the immortality of the character below and the death of many others. The story overall carries a sad theme as the two friends are seperated and eventually become enemies. I wanted to convey those emotions through the expressions of the characters as well as the distraught poses
