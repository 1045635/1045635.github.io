Inspiration for my Art:

The Rednote/Tiktok user zhishiwaan is my biggest inspiration for my art, especially because of their technique in drawing as well as overall artstyle. I admire the way that they draw faces in a way that makes each drawing have a delicate feel to it. The way they color also inspires me because their understanding of colors allows them to create atmospheres that really immerses the viewer into the art. Most of my art style is based off of their own art as they're currently my biggest inspiration.
